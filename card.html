Talpidae

Talpidae is a stronger version of mega mole, both exclusive to Elona+.

  • Scientific Name: Talpidae
  • Average Length: 12-15 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 3-6 years
  • Habitat: Woodland, grassland and farmland

Besides the wall-digging ability of the mega mole, talpidae also uses Scavenge (loots backpack, heals self by consuming your edible items), and occasionally casts Super Gravity (gravity status effect+physical damage).
